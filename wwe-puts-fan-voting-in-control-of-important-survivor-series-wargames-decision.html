WWE Puts Fan Voting in Control of Important Survivor Series WarGames Decision

Publish date: 2024-09-05

WWE’s 2023 Survivor Series event is coming at fans in a big way, and this week’s SmackDown included a few interesting angles that built toward the show. Now, a big part of the night will be up to the fans.

Ringside News has comprehensive coverage of WWE SmackDown’s November 17th episode. It was quite show, and the closing segment revealed Becky Lynch as the babyface team’s 4th Survivor Series partner.

WWE has made a significant announcement regarding the involvement of fans in the upcoming Women’s WarGames match at WWE Survivor Series 2023.

The announcement has generated anticipation and excitement within the WWE community. WWE recently unveiled the lineup for the Women’s WarGames match, which is slated for November 25th in Chicago. The match will feature Becky Lynch, Bianca Belair, Charlotte Flair, and Shotzi teaming up to take on Damage CTRL’s Bayley, IYO SKY, Kairi Sane, and Asuka within the WarGames structure. Now, fans can vote for which team will get an advantage in the match. The men are wrestling for that privilege on RAW next week.

As we approach the highly anticipated Survivor Series 2023 in Chicago, excitement is building to unprecedented levels. WWE’s innovative approach promises to make the Women’s WarGames match a truly interactive experience.

Fans will get to determine who will have the advantage in that match by way of a vote. The fan vote will take place on WWE.com.

We will have more updates on this story, and so many more, here at Ringside News. As always, keep checking back for constant updates.
