About Moana

“Moana” is a 2016 animated Disney film set in ancient Polynesia. The story follows Moana, the daughter of the chief, who is chosen by the ocean to embark on a quest to restore Te Fiti’s heart.

Despite her father’s objections, Moana sets sail and, joined by Maui and her pet rooster Heihei, discovers her strength and identity along the way.

The film was acclaimed for its animation, music, and vocal performances, resonating with audiences worldwide and garnering Academy Award nominations, grossing over $682 million globally.

Moana Plot

“Moana” tells the story of Moana, chosen by the ocean to restore Te Fiti’s heart, stolen by the demigod Maui, leading to blight and darkness. Despite challenges, Moana perseveres, with the film celebrating themes of self-discovery and bravery.
