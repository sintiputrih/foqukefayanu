UFC 250 fight purses and bonuses revealed including Amanda Nunes vs Felicia Spencer plus Cody Garbra

Publish date: 2024-09-25

AMANDA NUNES earned a bumper payday as she retained her featherweight title at UFC 250 against Felicia Spencer.

The 32-year-old Brazilian made a cool $350,000 (£276,000) just for stepping in the Octagon for the main event at the APEX facility in Las Vegas, Nevada.

And she picked up another $100,000 (£79,000) for retaining her belt against her Canadian rival.

Her 29-year-old opponent bagged $125,000 (£99,000) for her appearance in the main event.

Meanwhile on the card, Cody Garbrandt will make $130,000 (£103,000) against Rafael Assuncao, who got $79,000 (£62,000).

Aljamain Sterling made $76,000 (£60,000) for his appearance at UFC 250, alongside Cory Sandhagen, who made $80,000 (£63,000).

Nunes - who also has the bantamweight title - defended the featherweight strap for the first time in the Vegas pay-per-view.

ESPN reporter Brett Okamoto said: "Some disclosed fight purses from tonight: Nunes 350k to show, 100k to win.
