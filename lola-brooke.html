Lola Brooke Career

Lola Brooke is a popular American rapper and musical artist. She started writing music when she was eight years old.

In 2016, she began making music with her cousin Jah, who taught her how to make beats using Pro Tools. That same year, she signed with Team 80 Productions.

In 2021, Lola Brooke had a big breakthrough with her single “Don’t Play with It,” which first became viral on Twitter and later on TikTok and Instagram. Her music style combines hip-hop and Brooklyn drill.

She got her stage name by combining “Lola Bunny” from Looney Tunes and “Brooklyn.” Her musical influences include 50 Cent, Meek Mill, and Lil Wayne.

In March 2023, Lola Brooke released both “So Disrespectful” and a remix of “Don’t Play with It,” featuring American rappers Latto and Yung Miami.

She also made an appearance on the CBS television series East New York. In the same year, she signed a deal with Arista Records.
