Aaron Carter: Hilary Duff is the Love of my Life

Publish date: 0001-01-01

Hilary Duff has been separated from ex-husband Mike Comrie for about 30 seconds and she already has guys lining up for her. And we’re not just talking any old guy…

According to US Weekly, Aaron Carter is turning back the clock to 2000 when he dated fellow-Disney sensation Hilary Duff. The couple dated for three years as teenagers until rumors spread that Carter cheated on Duff with Disney counterpart Lindsay Lohan. Apparently, Carter posted a modern-day picture of Duff on Twitter with the following caption:

“Don’t be that stupid douche that loses the love of your life forever.. Like me…I’ll spend the rest of my life trying to better myself to get back to her,” Carter, 26, continued. “I don’t care what ANY of you think.”

Sounds rather… unstable. Carter doesn’t appear to be taking the Twitter comment back either. It also doesn’t appear as if Duff is interested in Carter at the moment. She’s definitely separated from Comrie, but the two have remained close. They were just seen on vacation with their son Luca, 23 months, after they were separated.
