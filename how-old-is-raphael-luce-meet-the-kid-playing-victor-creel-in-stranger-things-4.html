How Old Is Raphael Luce? Meet The Kid Playing Victor Creel In Stranger Things 4

Publish date: 2024-07-31

Raphael Luce is a Franco-American born in Vermont and raised in France for most of his life. Raphael plays Henry Creel, son of Victor Creel, in season 4 of The Stranger Things. What is Raphael Luce’s Age? Let’s find out. 

On December 29, 2005, Raphael Luce was born to Jade Kindar-Martin and Karine Mauffrey in Burlington, VT. Raphael is the oldest among three children. His brothers Gabriel Sky and Jophielle Love are also actors.

He and his siblings started a page called Follow the Halos. The main motive of that page was to encourage people to act on the 17 Global Goals, which the U.N sets to make the world a better place.

Raphael Luce Wikipedia And Bio Explored

Raphael’s family works either as actors or stunt performers in the movie industry. His sister, Jophielle Love, plays the role of Violet in General Hospital. His brother is Gabriel Sky, who played Diego in Seberg.

His parents are Jade Kindar-Martin and Karine Mauffrey. Both are stunt performers and former Cirque du Soleil artists.

Raphael is bilingual. He can speak French and English fluently. He splits his time between Los Angeles, New Orleans, Atlanta, and the south of France. He performs as a stuntman, as he is a very active kid.

On top of acting, he hopes to make a career in the stunt industry. He hopes to create content that inspires kids and adults to be more creative and care for humanity.

Stranger Things 4’s Victor Creel

Victor Creel is the character portrayed by Robert Englund, the recurring character in the fourth season of Stranger Things.

This character was revealed on November 20, 2020, via the show’s official social media accounts and several other new characters.

According to the show’s Wikipedia page, Raphael is playing the role of the younger version of Henry Creel, son of Victor Creel.

How Old Is Raphael Luce?

Raphael is a very energetic kid and is very athletic all around. He has done stunts training with famous stunt actor John Moio. He has also been a circus performer. He has acquired various skills such as juggling, walking ball, high wire walking, hand balancing, parkour, etc.

Currently, he is 17 years old. His birthday falls on December 29 of every year. He has been extremely talented and hardworking since his childhood. If he continues his hustle in this way, he will be one of the most successful actors soon.

Raphael Luce Parents

Raphael is son of Jade Kindar-Martin and Karine Mauffrey. Both of his parents are stunt performers. According to Wikipedia, His father started performing on the wire at 14 with Circus Smirkus, a youth circus based in Vermont, USA.

He is also known for doing World Record-setting double skywalk across the River Thames in London in 1997 with his friend and business partner Didier Paquette.

Raphael’s parents uniquely married in 2004. They tied their knot high up in the air on a wire stretched over the grounds of the Château de Chantilly in Chantilly, France.
