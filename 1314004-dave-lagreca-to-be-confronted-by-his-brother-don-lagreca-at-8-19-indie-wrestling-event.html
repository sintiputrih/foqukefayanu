Dave LaGreca To Be Confronted By His Brother Don LaGreca At 8/19 Indie Wrestling Event

Publish date: 2024-06-14

The host of Sirius XM’s Busted Open Radio, , will be confronted by his brother Don LaGreca of The Michael Kay Show this Friday night at ISPW in New Jersey.

The following is a press release from ISPW regarding their upcoming event on August 19:

The Michael Kay Show’s Don LaGreca will be appearing live this Friday night at the ISPW Summertime Blues show at St. Anthony’s Church Gym in Butler, NJ to confront his brother Dave LaGreca, the host of Sirius XM’s Busted Open Radio.

The show will be headlined by a TLC Match for the ISPW Tag Team Titles when The Birds of The Sun (Leo Sparrow & GKM) defend against The Now and Crowbar & Percival Payne.

The new ISPW World Heavyweight Champion Justin Corino (with his manager former WWE Tough Enough Champion Maven) will defend the title for the first time against Traxx.

In other action packed matches: Bull James defends the ISPW Tri-State Championship against Rick Recon (with Dave LaGreca & Miss Deville), “The Gift of Gab” Gabby Ortiz defends the ISPW Women’s Championship against Vicious Vicki in a No Holds Barred Match, “The Superstar” Danny Morrison takes on Shawn Donavan, Afa Jr battles Dominick DeNaro, and “Main Event” Michael Mars goes one on one with TJ Epixx (with HC Loc)

There will also be special appearances by WWE Legends Virgil and Zeb Colter.
