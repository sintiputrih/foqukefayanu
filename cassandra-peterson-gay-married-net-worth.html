Cassandra Peterson Came Out As A Gay

Publish date: 2024-08-21

Cassandra Peterson, aka Elvira, announced in her newly released memoir, Yours Cruelly, Elvira: Memoirs of the Mistress of the dark, that she had a relationship of 19 years with a woman named Teresa T. Wierson, who became her friend, assistant, and eventually a longtime love. She shared Wierson had an entry when she was emotionally and sexually closed for business after her divorce.

At the time, they both were divorced, and when her daughter went to her father’s house, the pair went on a movie date and fell for each other. Peterson wrote that she was with someone who made her feel safe, blessed, and truly loved for the first time in her life.

Peterson had once talked about how she mistook her for a man, a real bad guy who gave intense energy when they first met at the Hollywood Gold’s Gym.

Her reason for keeping the relationship secret was not to disappoint her fans as she clarifies,

“ I’m happy and relieved to finally allow our secret to see the light of day. So I was worried that if I announced I was no longer living the straight life, my fans would feel lied to, call me hypocrite , and abandon me.”

She opened up about how she fell in love with Teresa, an androgynous creature who appeared on her doorstep when she was desperate.

Cassandra Peterson's Net worth

Cassandra Peterson, best known as Elvira, is an American TV personality who has an estimated net worth of $3 million. She gained more popularity and attention after becoming gothic goddess Elvira, Mistress of the dark on KHJ’s weekly show Elvira’s Movie Macabre aired from 1981 to1986.

Cassandra signed a contract to become a showgirl in Viva Les Girls at the Dunes in Las Vegas at the age of 17. After getting advice from Elvis Presley, she was also a lead singer of the Italian rock band Latins 80 and The Snails. She also learned acting and comedic skills when she joined the Los Angeles troupe ‘The Groundlings’ in 1979.

Peterson got to work with the producers of the horror show Fright Night, where she created the sexy vampish look of her character Elvira. Her character became a craze and a brand when the show was aired for five seasons. The character was associated with many Halloween costumes and decors, comic books, perfumes, dolls, etc. While playing the character in ‘Elvira’s Movie Macabre’ her costume was of black gown and black wig using a tone of California ’valley girl.’ She used sharp and sarcastic jokes and comments.

Cassandra Peterson had starred in various movies such as Roma (1972), It’s My Party (1996), Pee-wee’s Big Adventure (1985), Jekyll and Hyde... Together Again (1982) and others. Her famous movie was released in 1988 named Elvira: Mistress of the Dark. Her movies are mainly based on dark comedy and the Sci-fi genre.
