JaMarr Chase contract, salary and net worth explored

Publish date: 2024-06-06

Ja’Marr Anthony Chase is an American football wide receiver for the National Football League’s Cincinnati Bengals. Chase attended LSU and won the Fred Biletnikoff Award and the 2020 College Football Playoff National Championship as a sophomore.

Table of Content

Contract

Ja’Marr Chase signed a four-year, $30.8 million contract with the Cincinnati Bengals.

Salary per year

Ja’Marr Chase earns $7,704,910 annually.

Net worth

Ja’Marr Chase has a net worth of $35 million.

Chase had 159 yards and a 70-yard touchdown at the end of the first half in the Bengals’ 25-22 overtime loss to the Green Bay Packers, earning him another Rookie of the Week award. Chase finished with 201 receiving yards, including an 82-yard touchdown, in the Bengals’ 41-17 win over the Baltimore Ravens in Week 7, earning him his first AFC Offensive Player of the Week award. Chase’s 754 receiving yards set an NFL record for the most by a player in their first seven career games. Chase was named to the Pro Bowl in 2022 on December 22, 2018.
