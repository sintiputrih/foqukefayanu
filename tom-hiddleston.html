Tom Hiddleston News - Us Weekly

Publish date: 2024-03-14

Name:Tom Hiddleston

Hometown:London, England

Birthday:February 9, 1981

About

Tom Hiddleston is an English actor, born February 9, 1981 in Westminster, London. His career breakthrough came when he was cast as Loki in the Marvel Cinematic Universe, appearing in Thor (2011), The Avengers (2012), Thor: The Dark World (2013), and Thor: Ragnarok (2017). Hiddleston also starred in TV miniseries The Night Manager in 2016.

In his personal life, Hiddleston was widely known for his relationship with Taylor Swift, which was confirmed in July 2016 and ended in September 2016. The actor began dating Zawe Ashton in 2019, and Us Weekly confirmed the couple’s engagement in March 2022.
