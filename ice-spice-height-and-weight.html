Career Beginnings

After meeting a record producer named RiotUSA in 2021 when both were students at the State University of New York at Purchase, Ice Spice began rapping.

After a video of Ice Spice completing the “Buss It” challenge went viral on Twitter, he produced her debut single, “Bully Freestyle,” which was made available in March 2021.

She became well-known on Instagram when her song “Name of Love” acquired popularity on SoundCloud.

With a video and an August 10, 2022 release, her song “Munch (Feelin’ U)” was likewise produced by RiotUSA and distributed by WorldStarHipHop.

Drake’s support helped the song acquire fame when he aired it on his Sirius XM radio station, Sound 42.

Following its virality on Twitter and TikTok, it debuted on the Hot R&B/Hip-Hop Songs and Bubbling Under Hot 100 lists of Billboard.

The song “One Time” by B-Lovee featured Ice Spice as a featured artist in September 2022.

She inked a record deal with 10K Projects and Capitol Records later that month.

She released the single “Bikini Bottom” on October 28.

The singles “Munch (Feelin’ U),” “Bikini Bottom,” and “In Ha Mood” were included on Ice Spice’s debut EP, Like..?, which was released on January 20, 2023.
