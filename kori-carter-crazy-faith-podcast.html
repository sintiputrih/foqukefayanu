World Champion Kori Carter is like Mike running for Jordan Brand | Crazy Faith Podcast | Episode 8

Kori Carter is one of the most interesting athletes in track and field and was the 400MH World Champion in 2017. In her illustrious career Kori is a nine-time All-American at Stanford and also is one of the few track and field athletes sponsored by Jordan Brand. In this episode Jasmine Todd sits down with Kori to discuss a variety of topics including Covid-19 , Animals and the 2020 Tokyo Olympics
