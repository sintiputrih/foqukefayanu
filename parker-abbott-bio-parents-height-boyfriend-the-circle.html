Meet Parker Abbott aka Paul On The Circle

Netflix’s The Circle season 4 premiered on May 4, 2022,  and as always there stood 9 new players including Parker Abbott competing for that $150 thousand grand prize either by appearing as themselves or as their catfish personality.

On the game show, the contestants are allowed to create friendships and flirtations with people in the game without ever telling them who they really are. So, the theme is that by the end of the reach round all players must rate each other through a popularity-based ranking process. Thus, whoever’s at the top they’ll be labeled “influencers” in the group, and those “influencers” have the power to decide who is going to be eliminated next.

Upon getting eliminated, the person’s true identity gets revealed to the other players in the game!

Parker Abbott joined The Circle 2022 as her father Paul, a 56-year-old married man with two children. “(He’s) like someone you will put your trust and loyalty (into),” she explained her catfish persona.

Unfortunately, though she planned on “being the most manipulative cutthroat player,” she couldn’t just get into the character of a 56-year-old man. Moreover, while interacting with Frank Grimsley, though Parker was sure that his mindset would completely match hers in the real world, she never got around to getting Frank as her ally.

Eventually, finding herself failing to get a permanent ally who would have supported her throughout, she was voted out at the end of ep 2.

Fans, amusingly, didn’t find it surprising that Parker was eliminated as they believed Parker did a poor job impersonating her father. “I wish Parker had gone in as herself. She created too many loopholes in Paul’s personality,” a fan tweeted.
