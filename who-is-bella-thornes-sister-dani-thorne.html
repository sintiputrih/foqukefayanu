Considering that celebrities are human beings just like the rest of us, it shouldn’t come as a surprise to anyone that some stars have similarly complicated relationships with their siblings. Worse yet, since many people are so interested in stars these days, when a celebrity’s siblings do something negative it reflects on their famous brother or sister. The Thorne Family

During the ‘90s, Delancey and Tamara Thorne had four children together, Bella, Kalli, Remy, and Dani. Unfortunately for the family, the Thorne patriarch suddenly lost his life due to a traffic accident that took place in 2007. According to what Bella Thorne has said during interviews, her dad’s passing left the Thorne kids to be raised by their single mother. Of course, bringing up four kids is extremely expensive so it is understandable that the Thorne family struggled to get by on a single income for a while.

In order to help her family get by, Bella Thorne began an acting career at a very young age. Similarly, Dani Thorne has revealed that she began working as a model and doing commercials when she was only 4-years-old. After Bella Thorne became a child star, her family no longer needed to fear homelessness but that doesn’t mean that the Thorne kids stopped working. On top of her career saving her family financially, Bella Thorne has been able to help others with her wealth through philanthropy.

Since Bella Thorne left the Disney Channel behind, it is very safe to say that she has done many things that her former bosses would disapprove of. For example, since leaving the House of Mouse behind, Bella Thorne has made a living appearing in more mature films including The Babysitter and its 2020 sequel The Babysitter: Killer Queen. Another Entertainer

When the Thorne family is brought up, there is no doubt that Bella is the one who comes to mind first for the vast majority of people and that is for good reason. Despite the success that Bella Thorne has enjoyed during her career, she isn’t the only member of her family that has gone into the entertainment industry. For example, Bella’s sister Kaili, her brother Remy, and her other sister Dani all have IMDb pages because each of them has several acting credits.

In the case of Dani Thorne, she left acting in the rearview mirror a number of years ago as she has chosen to focus on launching a music career. Operating as a music producer and DJ, Dani has an alter ego and goes by the name COM3T. Additionally, Dani and someone named Dylan Jetson formed an electronic music duo that went under the name Master of the People. According to womenofedm.com, Master of the People “keep the energy high, the movement fast-paced, and on your toes for the genre switch-ups. Mixing between hard electro, trap, dubstep, and future bass, Master of the People takes you on a ride.”

On a personal level, it seems like Dani and Bella Thorne have a really good relationship. After all, Bella has shown up at several of Dani’s gigs so she could support her sister’s musical efforts. Additionally, Bella and Dani are so close that they like to call themselves twins, which also is pretty fitting since they look so much alike. Since the sisters are so close and have that nickname for themselves, they got matching tattoos that read “If lost, find twin”.
