Hayley Atwell – Bio, Age

After graduating from high school, she was on the road for two years, working with a casting director alongside her father. In a bid to hone her acting skills, Hayley became an intern at the Guildhall School of Music and Drama, a private theater institution where she completed a degree in acting.
