Rita Ora Shines in a Beaded Dress at Premiere of Fianc Taika Waititis Film Thor: Love And Thund

Publish date: 2024-06-08

Attending the premiere of fiancé Taika Waititi’s film Thor: Love And Thunder, Rita Ora looked resplendent.

She showed off a plunging beaded dress to support her filmmaker boyfriend at London’s Leicester Square on July 5.

The 46-year-old Kiwi director has been in a relationship with pop star Rita, 31, since early last year.

Reports have surfaced that the pair are engaged and planning to wed this summer, although her engagement ring was concealed by her sleeve at the event.
