Meet HGTV Calvin LaMont! Bio, Age, Net Worth, Married, Buy It Or Build It

Publish date: 2024-02-10

HGTV’s latest home renovation series Buy It or Built It premiered on 18 May 2022 encircling the forever dilemma of whether it is better to buy a home or build a home from the ground up. The series stars Dallas-based twin brothers and contractors Chris and Calvin LaMont as they engage in a little friendly competition to help clients figure out if they should renovate an existing home or build a new one to fit their needs.

Now, in the rest of the writing, we shall tell you all about one of the twins Calvin LaMont, his age, net worth, Buy It Or Build It stint, and everything.

Calvin LaMont On HGTV’s Buy It Or Build It

HGTV’s Buy It Or Build It features Calvin LaMont and his brother trying to help clients find their dream homes either by buying and renovating an existing home or starting from scratch by building a new one.

During each hour-long episode, of the six-part series, Calvin likes the clean slate and lack of boundaries and therefore prefers building from the ground up. His brother on the other hand tries to find properties with flipping potential. And fortunately, this difference in opinion only makes them a special team and the perfect hosts for the show.

Every time a client comes to them on Buy It or Build It, Chris’s task is to look for properties that have potential. Then, based on the clients’ budget, he will share how he can give the home an updated look to suit their needs. While Calvin will present the clients with designs for a new build within their budget. And when the clients choose to buy or build their dream home, the LaMont brothers are ready to make things happen for them.

The twin brothers have been flipping homes since at least 2015. They moved to Dallas in 2010, and the movement also kick-started their real estate career. But, this was certainly not their first choice. When they first arrived in Texas, the brothers had opened up a janitorial and floor cleaning company.

Fast forward to now, they already own more than 25 residential lots. They have taken their building business to impressive heights, getting the most excited about up-and-coming neighborhoods and focusing on locations with potential.

As for the future, they have plans of taking their business to cities outside of Texas, including St. Louis and Augusta, Georgia.

Calvin LaMont Net Worth

Calvin LaMont and his identical twin were believed to have an estimated combined net worth of $5 million, as of 2022.

On his LinkedIn, Calvin proudly tells he has owned and operated his own company CWC Clean With Care since 2009. He also mentions being the CEO and Founder of Volume Income Properties Inc., a real estate development, as of May 2009.

Before that from 2003 through 2007, Calvin studied BA in Business Information Systems at Lehigh University College of Business.

Is Calvin LaMont Married Yet?

Calvin LaMont did not seem to have already tied the knot (as of 2022). But, he sure was committed relationship with Sabrina Boxton. This lady with beautiful long curls also is Calvin’s baby mama as together they have a daughter who turned 3 years old on 15 May 2022.

On 14 December 2021, Calvin announced his engagement to his “beautiful queen” on Instagram. He then detailed how amazing the family Vacation to his country of Jamaica was. He said he has been to Jamaica more times than he can count, but this one, when he got engaged, was the most memorable time ever. At the time, he had had his aunties, uncles, and cousins come over to Jamaica to celebrate his dad’s 63rd birthday and grandmother’s 87th birthday. And to make this day even more memorable, he had asked his baby mama to marry him and she of course had said “Yes!”.

Calvin LaMont Age

Calvin LaMont was born in 1985. So, he reached the age of 37 in 2022.
