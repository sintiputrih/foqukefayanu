Biography

Rachel Cruze was born in Tennessee on April 25, 1989. Known for her prolific career in speaking and writing on the importance of financial literacy. In 2014, her book that she wrote alongside her father Dave Ramsey, Smart Money, Smart Kids, earned its place atop the New York Times’ best-seller list. She interviewed Dr. Ben Carson for her vlog series on her 199th episode on September 16, 2015. She began motivationally speaking on the importance of financial literacy during her mid-teens. She subsequently graduated from the University of Tennessee with her Bachelor of Arts degree in communication studies.
