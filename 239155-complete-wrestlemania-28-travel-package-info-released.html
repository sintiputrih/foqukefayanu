Complete WrestleMania 28 Travel Package Info Released

Publish date: 2024-06-24

Devin Cutting has sent in the following:

Hello WWE Universe!!

WWE’s most electrifying event is coming to Miami and you can experience it in style with WrestleMania XXVIII Travel Packages!  Be there for the most anticipated match in WWE history when WWE Superstar John Cena and Dwayne “The Rock” Johnson face off at Sun Life Stadium at WrestleMania XXVIII!

For our most loyal fans, you are receiving the package information and pricing before it is released to the public.  See below for detailed pricing and package descriptions. 

 

New this year – get guaranteed ringside seats in the first 4 rows with our brand new VIP Package!  Also included with the VIP Package, an exclusive VIP stadium entrance – arrive in style and walk the black carpet like WrestleMania royalty.  VIP guests enjoy the comfort and convenience of their very own stadium entrance for use by VIP guests ONLY.  Also, upon arrival at the stadium, you will be greeted by a WWE Superstar, Diva or Legend welcoming you on-site.  You’ll kickoff your evening with this opportunity to snap a photo and capture your moment with a past or present WWE icon.  Limited quantities available.

 

Also this year, as an added benefit included with all WrestleMania XXVIII Travel Packages, round-trip bus transportation will be provided between your hotel location and WrestleMania XXVIII at Sun Life Stadium.

Get ready … WrestleMania XXVIII Travel Packages go on sale to the general public Monday, July 18th at 3:00pm ET.  Take part in an exclusive presale Monday, July 18th at 12:00pm ET for those on the WrestleMania Packages mailing list.  Then check your inbox on Monday, July 18th for an exclusive link to the presale and get your WrestleMania XXVIII Travel Packages prior to the general public!

WrestleMania XXVIII Travel Packages are on a first-come first-serve basis so don’t delay … mark your calendars now!

See you at WrestleMania XXVIII in Miami!!!
