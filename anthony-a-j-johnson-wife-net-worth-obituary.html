Anthony “A.J.” Johnson Family

Anthony “A.J.” Johnson was born to his parents in Compton, California, United States. In his perspective, growing up in Compton was tough. As he shared, if a person could make out of Compton, then the person could make it to anywhere.

He grew up in the ambiance of drive-by shooting every night, so, one always had to watch his back. According to him, “you don’t know which care are coming to say “Hi” or which car are coming to shoot, you had to be really careful and watch yourself back, back in them days”

During that time, there was always a fight at the school. He was a member of a Crip gang (since the age of 16) and every day in the school he used to go to the football field to fight with the members of the Blood gang. He got stabbed once but never got shot ever in his life.

Anthony’s father was a stunt man. He co-founded the Black Stuntmen’s Association. His father worked as a stunt man when he was in school. Back in the day, White Stuntmen were taken to the Black actors. After his father saw that, he and seven of his friends protested that.
