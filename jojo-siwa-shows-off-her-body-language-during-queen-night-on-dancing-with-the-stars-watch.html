JoJo Siwa Shows Off Her Body Language During Queen Night on Dancing with the Stars W

Publish date: 2024-09-07

JoJo Siwa is once again slaying Dancing with the Stars!

The 18-year-old Nickelodeon star and Dance Moms alum once again took over the dance floor with partner Jenna Johnson during Queen Night on Monday (November 1).

Click inside to watch her performance!

For her performance, JoJo and Jenna slipped into coordinating, sparkling bodysuits while they performed the Tango to Queen‘s song “Body Language.”

JoJo‘s dance once again wowed the judges.

Derek Hough called her dance “avant garde” and “experimental” while Bruno Tonioli thought her “supermodel tango” was “slinky, sexy, and cool.”

In the end, the Carrie Ann Inaba, Bruno, and Derek gave her 10s while Len Goodman gave her a 9, earning her a 39 out of 40.
