    Elena Kampouris Career

    Elena made her acting debut with a small guest role as a Constance Girl in one episode of The CW teen drama series Gossip Girl in 2012. This was followed by the Nickelodeon television comedy film Jinxed (2013), in which she played Ivy Murray. In the same year, she made her film debut in the drama Labor Day, playing a young Rachel McCann, with Kate Winslet and Josh Brolin. Elena signed with the William Morris Endeavor talent agency in December 2013.

    Elena played Allison Doss in the comedy-drama film Men, Women & Children in 2014. Later, she portrayed Alexia in the comedy-drama film The Cobbler, alongside Adam Sandler and Dustin Hoffman.

    Elena starred in the NBC thriller series American Odyssey as Maya Decker in 2015, the daughter of Peter Facinelli’s character. After one season the series was canceled.  Elena had a supporting role in the romantic comedy film My Big Fat Greek Wedding 2, playing the daughter of Nia Vardalos and John Corbett’s characters.

    Elena made her Broadway debut in a production of Les Liaisons Dangereuses, also starring Liev Schreiber and Janet McTeer in 2016. She played the supporting role of Cécile Volanges, a wealthy and naive teenager. In  2017 she had a supporting role as Juliet Sykes in the drama film Before I Fall, alongside Zoey Deutch and Logan Miller.

    It was announced on February 11, 2019, that Kampouris was cast as Chloe Sampson in the upcoming Netflix superhero series, Jupiter’s Legacy.
