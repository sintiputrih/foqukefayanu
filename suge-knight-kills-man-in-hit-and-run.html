Suge Knight Kills Man in Hit and Run: Report

Publish date: 2024-08-02

Death Row records co-founder Suge Knight allegedly killed a man in a hit and run on a movie set in Compton, Calif., on Thursday.

TMZ identifies the victim as Terry Carter, a friend of Knight’s. Another man – an actor named Cle “Bone” Sloane – was also struck, and suffered unspecified injuries.

Sources say Knight fled the scene after striking Carter and Sloane.

The incident occurred on a set for a movie or music video. Rappers The Game, Ice Cube, and Dr. Dre were reportedly present.

Witnesses say Knight argued with two crew members before getting into his car and speeding off.

This is just the latest in a recent string of troubles for the hip hop mogul.

In October, the mogul was arrested alongside comedian Katt Williams, and charged with robbery following an incident involving an aggressive paparazzo.

Prior to that, Suge was shot six times at a party hosted by troubled R&B artist Chris Brown.

Insiders claim Knight sped off from the scene because he feared a physical confrontation due to his “frail health” resulting from being shot in August.

Police have not issued a statement about today’s incident, but it is believed that a warrant has been issued for Knight’s arrest.
