Couples Still Together And Strong

Publish date: 2024-09-12

Seeking Sister Wife is a reality TV show that documents the lives of different families who seek new sister wives in their lives. While there are quite a number of families that have appeared on the show, only a few are still together as couples. Some of the couples that are still together include The Winders Family, The Alldredges Family, The Merrifields Family, The Clarks Family, The Joneses Family, and The Epps Family.

New sister wives are usually introduced into the plural relationship by the husband and the first wife. There are instances whereby the relationship or marriage shatters while there are some that continue to wax stronger.

Table of Contents

Meet Seeking Sister Wife Couple that is Still Together

1. The Merrifield

Dannielle and Garrick Merrield made their debut on season 3 of the show. The couple was looking to expand their family when they first appeared on the show. They wanted to marry Roberta, a Brazilian but Roberta could not move to the United States unless she had a K-1 visa. In other to get that, Garrick would have to marry Roberta legally.

For that to happen, Dannielle had to file for a divorce from Garrick. So while they were not legally married, they were spiritually married.

By the time the family returned for season 4, they were looking forward to adding a woman called Lea into their brood. Unfortunately, the relationship with Lea did not work out as expected. Also, Garrick’s Brazilian wife broke up with the family via text message.

As of 2022, the Merrields are already dating a mystery Mexican woman whom they have plans of meeting later in the year.

2. The Joneses

Sidian and Tosha Jones joined Seeking Sister Wife in season 2. Sidian’s first wife had already left the family so Tosha is now the first wife. While their relationship has been dubbed very rocky, the couple shared their journey on season 4 of the show in June 2022.

The couple revealed that they were dating a woman from the Philippines called Arielle. Recently, the family revealed that Tosha, Sidian, and their kids traveled to the Philippines to spend time with Arielle while they are still working on getting her a K-1 visa.

3. The Winders

The Winders are Colton Winder, his first wife Tami, and his second wife Sophie. The family made their debut in 2019 on season 2 of the show. The polygamous family already had two children when they were set to welcome another wife. The potential wife, Kimberley Greene did not relate well with the two sister wives and their husbands.

The family did not return for season 4 in 2022 but was open to adding more wives.

4. The Alldredges

Jeff Alldredge appeared in the first season of Sister’s Wife with his two wives, Vanessa and Sharis. During the first season, the family dated a woman called Melina but the relationship ended before season two premiered. The family returned for the season with a potential sister wife, Jennifer. That did not end well either hence they did not come back for the third season of the show.

However, the Alldredge family announced via Facebook that they had new additions to their family in July 2020. The new sister wife, Donna joined the family with her daughter Evelyn. That same year, Sharis revealed to their fans that she was pregnant with her daughter, Chloe Vienna Esther Alldredge.

Later on June 2, 2021, the reality TV family revealed via social media that they had been physically separated from Donna. Since then, the family has not given any updates about their family ever since.

5. The Clarks

Jarod Clark and his wife Vanessa joined the show in season 3 when they were already courting Kaleh. However, Jarod later ended things with Kaleh and moved on to another woman, Emily. That did not go well either.

Although they are yet to return for season 4, the couple is still together just that they have not added a sister wife yet.

6. The Epps Family

The Epps Family comprises Marcus Epps and Taryn. The pair were college sweethearts. However, the marriage ended after 10 years. While they were separated for a while, Marcus met Inda Epps, and they even got engaged.

Interestingly, Taryn decided to reconcile with Marcus and so she moved back. Marcus on the other hand did not want to lose India so he suggested that they start a polygamous family and the women all agreed.

The family made their debut on the show in season 4. During the show, two potential sister wives Bina and Janae were introduced. But before the end of the season, the family revealed they wanted to end the relationship with the two women.

Meanwhile, it was later revealed that Marcus continued dating both Bina and Janae even when his wives thought he had ended the relationship. Moreso, reports revealed that he had been dating other women as well. Although he already had plans to marry India in November 2022, Marcus was dating different women in-between time.

7. The Davis Family

Unlike the other families, The Davis family has a different structure entirely. Nick Davis, the family patriarch was not legally married to either of his two wives April and Jennifer. Instead, April Davis and Jennifer Davis were legally married to each other before they got involved with Nick and so decided to take his last name.

The trio made their debut on season 4 of Seeking Sister Wife with the intention of adding a third wife to their brood. Nick then courted a woman called Danielle and later proposed to her.

In the season 4 finale, the family revealed that Jennifer and Nick welcomed their first baby together on June 29, 2022. In other to accommodate the new baby and a new wife, the Davis family moved into a larger home.

According to reports, the latest update about the family was that they were making sleeping arrangements for Nick and all his three wives.

Danielle, who’s new in the four-person relationship, revealed she gets a little jealous sometimes but admits with time, she would learn to watch her man kiss other women besides her.

8. The Foleys

Steve Foley was already married to an unnamed woman when he decided to adopt the plural lifestyle with Brenda. Since the first wife did not buy the idea she filed for divorce and so Steve married Brenda who now became his first wife.

When the Foleys joined the show in season 4, they were courting a woman called April. Although April and Brenda had issues in the relationship, reports say they have moved past that are working on building a happy family together.

In an Update of the season 4 finale, it was revealed that Steve and Brenda were dating Steve’s high school prom date, as a new potential sister wife.

While these families are still together as of the time of this writing, it is uncertain if they would all be featured in season 5 of the show. As for families that expanded in season 4, it is expected that they would be featured. There are speculations that new families might be introduced as well.

The TLC show averaged around 600,000 viewers per episode in season 4 and it has remained one of the top 10 shows on TLC. While there is no news about season 5 yet, it is safe to assume that the plural relationships on the show would increase since several families from the previous seasons did not make it to the just concluded season.
