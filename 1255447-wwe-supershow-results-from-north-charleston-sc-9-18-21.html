WWE Live Event Results From North Charleston, SC (9/18/21)

Publish date: 2024-02-13

WWE was live in South Carolina last night for another Supershow featuring the top superstars of both RAW and SmackDown. Check out the full results from the show below. It should be noted that the matches will look much different than previous weeks as WWE runs shows in the UK also starting 9/19.

  • Keith Lee defeated Akira Tozawa
  • Riddle defeated AJ Styles
  • Jeff Hardy defeated Karrion Kross
  • WWE 24/7 Championship Match: Reggie defeated R-Truth
  • WWE SmackDown Tag Team Championships: The Usos defeated New Day
  • Rhea Ripley defeated Shayna Baszler
  • United States Championship: Damian Priest defeated Sheamus
  • WWE RAW Women’s Championship: Charlotte & Alexa Bliss wrestle to a double countout
  • Roman Reigns defeated Bobby Lashley & Big E in a triple threat match
