Indya Moore Responds to Filming Last Night's Episode of Pose

Publish date: 2024-08-29

Indya Moore is taking time to pay tribute to their Pose costars.

In an Instagram post on Sunday about filming the FX drama's most recent episode, Moore wrote about their experience working with Jace Moses and Angel Bismark Curiel.

In the sweet, effusive post, Moore wrote:

"Working with these two was very special. Jace is a beautifully blooming artist- with a promising, creative path. He is wise, and so made up of care, consideration. His sense of gratitude is so powerful- he inspires me to take a closer look at how I place value and what I value. I am so grateful for his contribution to our story, and membership in our pose family. Thank you for sharing your magic and helping us to show the world the family and love we all deserve.

I am so proud of Angel, his commitment to his craft, diligence, and ability to carry our story line. I said it before and ill say it again- working with Angel has made me a sharper artist and more thorough, patient, professional- by example no matter how challenging. Moments i felt like I couldn’t be better, or even identify how, there was Angel- growing and rising right before my eyes, pointing the way forward. Thank you for inspiring so much willpower, protecting & supporting me through vulnerable scenes, and for being such a great person to people- even when people aren’t always giving greatness back. Im excited to see how you continue to grow with and within your power.

Please enjoy tonight’s episode, Beautifully written and brilliantly directed by Janet Mock.
#PoseFX."

Pose airs Sundays at 10 p.m. ET/PT on FX.
