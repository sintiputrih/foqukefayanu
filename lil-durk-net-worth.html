Who is Lil Durk?

Lil Durk’s net worth is projected to be $8 million as of November 2022.

American rapper, singer, and composer Lil Durk hails from Chicago. He is the founder and main member of his own collective, Only the Family, as well as its record label.

Early Life

Durk Derrick Banks was born on October 19, 1992, in Chicago. Banks is a Chicago-based nurse and the son of Dontay Banks. His mother’s name is unknown. When he was just 7 years old, his father was sent to prison, thus he had to assume responsibility at a very young age.

In a magazine interview, Lil Durk recalled how his family never had enough food when he was a child. Lil Durk’s upbringing in a violent area had a significant impact on him.

Career, Net Worth

Lil Durk’s early professional years were spent working with the Chief Keef-endorsed Glo Gang label. However, the label never signed him. Lil Durk began to develop his own persona in the music industry as a result.

After his first two tracks were a success, Lil Durk put out a mixtape titled “Life Ain’t No Joke.” On the mixtape distribution website DatPiff, this mixtape was downloaded 216,000 times. The song “Life Ain’t No Joke” gained a lot of popularity thanks to social media platforms.

His career took a huge step forward in 2015 with the publication of his debut album, “Remember My Name.” His boyhood experiences with Chicago’s street violence are the subject of many of the songs on the album. The record, which peaked at number 14 on the Billboard 200 list, was a huge success.

Lil Durk’s sixth mixtape, titled “300 Days, 300 Nights,” was released that same year. To advertise this mixtape, the single “My Beyonce” was released.

His romantic interest Dej Loaf was featured in the song. This mixtape was praised by critics as a welcome change from his earlier albums, which mainly focused on the drug violence in the area where he was born.

On July 22, 2016, his second album, “Lil Durk 2X,” was made available. To generate interest in his second album, a single titled “She Just Wanna” with another rapper named “Ty Dolla Sign” was released two months prior to the album’s release. More people responded favorably to Durk’s second label than to his first.
