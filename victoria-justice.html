Victoria Justice News - Us Weekly

Publish date: 2024-06-10

Name:Victoria Justice

Hometown:Hollywood, FL

Birthday:February 19, 1993

About

Victoria Justice, a native of Hollywood, Fla., rose to fame as Tori Vega on Nickelodeon’s Victorious, originating the role in 2010 and assuming it until the series finale in 2013. Making her acting debut at age 10 in a Gilmore Girls episode, the actress has also appeared on Disney’s The Suite Life of Zack and Cody and Nickelodeon’s Zoey 101, starring Jamie-Lynn Spears.

Also a singer, Justice sang on the official soundtrack to TV’s Victorious, which includes the singles “Freak the Freak Out,” “Beggin’ On Your Knees” and the show’s theme, “Make It Shine.”

Honored with Teen Choice, Kids’ Choice, NAACP Image and ALMA award nominations, Justice most recently appeared in the film Fun Size, which costarred Chelsea Handler and Johnny Knoxville.
