Lilibet Lili Diana Mountbatten-Windsor News

Publish date: 2024-05-14

Name:Lilibet “Lili” Diana Mountbatten-Windsor

Hometown:Santa Barbara, California

Birthday:June 4, 2021

About

Lilibet “Lili” Diana Mountbatten-Windsor is the second child of Prince Harry and wife Meghan Markle. She was born in June 2021.

Lili was named after both the late Queen Elizabeth II and Princess Diana. Her Majesty’s family nickname is Lilibet, which is where Lili’s first name comes from. Her middle name is a nod to Harry’s late mother.

Meghan and Harry announced in February 2021 that they had baby No. 2 on the way. Ahead of Lili’s arrival, the Duke of Sussex gushed about the couple’s growing family during their CBS tell-all interview in March 2021, revealing the little one’s sex for the first time.

Lili, who has dual citizenship in both the U.S. and the U.K., is seventh in line for the British crown, following her older brother, Archie. Prince William and Princess Kate’s three children — Prince George, Princess Charlotte and Prince Louis — are also ahead of Harry and his two little ones.

Following the queen’s death in September 2022, Lilibet and Archie were granted the official royal titles of Princess and Prince of Sussex, respectively.
