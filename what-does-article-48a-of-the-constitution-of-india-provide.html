What does Article 48A of the Constitution of India provide?

Publish date: 2024-03-14

Article 48A

It reads as: The State shall endeavor to protect and improve the environment and to safeguard the forests and wildlife of the country. This Article was added by the 42nd Amendment, 1976 and places an obligation on the State to protect the environment and wildlife.
