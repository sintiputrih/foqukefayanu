7 Little Johnstons: Latest TV news, episodes and spoilers

Publish date: 2024-06-22
Who are the family members from 7 Little Johnstons?

Amber and Trent, and their five children – Jonah, Anna, Elizabeth, Emma, and Alex.

Do all of the 7 Little Johnstons have dwarfism?

Yes, both parents Amber and Trent and their five children have a form of dwarfism.

Where are the 7 Little Johnstons from?

The family hail from Georgia, where most still live and work.

Which of the 7 Little Johnstons kids are adopted?

Amber and Trent share biological children Jonah and Elizabeth. They then adopted three more kids who also have dwarfism from around the world. They adopted Anna from Russia, Alex who is South Korean and then Emma from China.
