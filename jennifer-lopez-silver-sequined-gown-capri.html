Jennifer Lopez Glitters in a Luxe Silver Sequined Gown in Capri

Publish date: 2024-02-16

Jennifer Lopez is wholeheartedly embracing la dolce vita.

Over the past week, J.Lo has been busy living her best life in Italy, where she is on vacation with husband Ben Affleck and their blended family. The excursion has also meant a constant turnout of some of the multi-hyphenate’s best summer looks yet, from sophisticated sundresses to itty-bitty string bikinis.

Most recently, Lopez’s holiday wardrobe took a luxe turn for a Saturday night out, when she was photographed partying at Capri’s famous Taverna Anema e Core. During the outing, she sang to a dancing crowd and banged on a tambourine while wearing a glittering long-sleeved gown completely covered in reflective silver sequins. She coordinated the look with more silver accessories, including a silver-and-gold Dolce & Gabbana box clutch and large dangling silver medallion earrings.

BACKGRID

Earlier in the trip, Lopez headed out for a boat ride with friends wearing a gorgeous white Zimmermann midi dress in a pastel pink-and-lavender watercolor floral print. The delicate piece featured long matching straps at the waist, which Lopez tied off into a large bow. To further accessorize, the “Let’s Get Loud” singer wore oversize sunglasses with a pale beige rim, gold chain bracelets, flower-shaped drop earrings, and a raffia purse by Ralph Lauren.

Later, she stepped out on a shopping spree clad in a glittery triangle bikini top from Calzedonia (on sale for $25), billowy white trousers from Ralph Lauren, a white cover-up top, a black Nick Fouquet hat, black Isabel Marant sunglasses, and a raffia tote bag from Brunello Cucinelli.
