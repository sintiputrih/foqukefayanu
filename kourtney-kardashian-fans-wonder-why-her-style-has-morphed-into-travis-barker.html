Ever since Kourtney Kardashian began dating her rockstar beau, fans have observed an obvious change in her fashion style. The reality television star appears to have adopted many of Travis Barker's fashion choices, which is making fans wonder why she is morphing into him.

Kardashian was recently slammed for changing her daughter's Penelope's style to match her boyfriend, and she is doing the same now!
