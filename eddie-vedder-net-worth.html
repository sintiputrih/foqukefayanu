Charity

EB Research Partnership (EBRP)

Vedder and his wife, Jill, are the co-founders of Epidermolysis Bullosa Research Partnership (ERBP).

It is a non-profit organization that works towards treating and curing EB, a skin disease that results in wounds that never heal. Similarly, the disease brings the onset of blisters, bleeding, defacement of skin, and persisting pain.

And, the foundation has helped many children dealing with that gruesome skin disorder.

To explain, in February 2021, Eddie collaborated with the artist Keegan Hall to raise $100K which was fully donated to EBRP. Moreover, the sum was raised in just an hour.

Career

Early Years

Eddie Vedder was born by the name Edward Louis Severson III in December 1964. Following his birth, his parents got divorced, and his mother remarried.

Subsequently, he left his tense home and joined high school but eventually dropped out. Having a passion for music, he returned to California in 1984 and started pursuing his passion by joining several bands.

Known for his impressive baritone vocals, Vedder joined a band named Bad Radio between 1988-1990.

It was the early 1990s when Eddie joined the American rock band named Pearl Jam which is believed to hype grunge music at that period.

The band’s original members were Eddie Vedder (lead vocalist), Stone Gossard (rhythm guitarist), Jeff Ament (bassist), Mike McCready (lead guitarist), and Dace Krusen (drummer).

Personal Life

Eddie Vedder was married to Beth Liebling in 1994, but the couple divorced after four years of marriage.

Then, in 2010, Vedder married his longtime lover, Jill McCormick holding a small ceremony in Oahu, Hawaii.

Also, the couple is blessed with two beautiful girls, Oliva and Harper Moon.
