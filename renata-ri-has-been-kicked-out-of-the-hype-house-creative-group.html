Renata Ri Has Been Kicked Out Of The Hype House Creative Group

Publish date: 2024-04-20

Fans of the TikTok creator were perplexed when Renata Ri was removed from the Hype House creative group. They deluged her most recent video with comments criticizing her omission from the new lineup. Ri responded to a remark by saying she had been “kicked out” of the program, but she didn’t elaborate. The 19-year-old has over 19.2 million TikTok followers under the moniker @riwww, where she produces lifestyle, lipsync, and POV videos. Renata Ri relocated from Russia to Los Angeles in order to grow her following on the app, and she joined the Hype House cast in December 2021.

Renata Ri is no longer a member of the Hype House ensemble.

On April 2, 2022, Renata Ri shared a humorous video with the caption:

“when you’re not sure how to dance”

The video was seen over 1.4 million times, and her admirers swamped the comment area, wondering why she wasn’t on the new Hype House roster.

“You are not in Hype House anymore?” one of her admirers said. “Yep, I was tossed out,” Ri answered. Ri does not just have a large number of TikTok followers. Her Instagram account has 1.3 million followers as well. On the short video platform, each of her videos may easily get over a million views. Fans were perplexed by her departure from the program after seeing all of these statistics.

Hype House manager Thomas Petrou and Reneta Ri, according to Dexerto, had a conflict of interest when the TikToker got into a dispute with one of the new Hype House members, Tabitha Swatosh. Ri was forced to quit the organization as a result of the event.

Tabitha Swatosh is a well-known TikTok creator

On Instagram, Hype House posted a photo of their new cast members. The group is made up of four original and four new members from TikTok’s influencer list. Thomas Petrou, Mia Hayward, Vinnie Hacker, and Jack Wright are among the founding members who remain in the Hype House. Sam Dezz, Brooke Monk, Tabitha Swatosh, and Jack Hayward will join them.

Swatosh is a well-known TikTok creator from Missouri. Miss Missouri State University, Miss Merry Christmas 2017, and other titles have been bestowed to the 21-year-old beauty pageant winner, YouTuber, and model. Her TikTok account has more than 9.3 million followers. She also has over 1.45 million followers on her YouTube channel, where she publishes vlogs and lifestyle videos.
