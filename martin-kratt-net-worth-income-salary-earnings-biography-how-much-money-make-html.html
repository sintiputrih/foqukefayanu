Martin William Kratt (born December 23, 1965) is an educational nature programme presenter from the United States. He and his younger brother Chris grew up in Warren Township, New Jersey, where they co-created the children’s television shows Kratts’ Creatures and Zoboomafoo, as well as the National Geographic Channel and Knowledge Network’s Be the Creature. They star in the animated television series Wild Kratts, which airs on PBS Kids Go!
