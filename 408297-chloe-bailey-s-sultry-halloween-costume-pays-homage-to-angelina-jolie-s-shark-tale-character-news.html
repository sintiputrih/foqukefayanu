Chloe Baileys Sultry Halloween Costume Pays Homage To Angelina Jolies Shark Tale Charact

Publish date: 2024-10-03

The 46-year-old actress had a role in six films that premiered that year, but the one Bailey chose to recreate was Lola from the Dreamworks animated film, Shark Tale. The “Have Mercy” singer put together a teensy tiny pink sequin tube top to emulate the fish’s scales with a coverup, hung low across her hips.

Bold pink and purple hair paired with matching eyeshadow and lipstick bring the whole look together. “She’s dangerous, supa bad. Better watch out she’ll take your cash,” the Atlanta native captioned the sultry snapshots, which has earned her praise from the likes of Megan Thee Stallion, Cardi B, and her little sister, Halle Bailey.

”Soo gooddd!!!!!!” Halle said about the costume “Omgggg,” Thee Stallion gassed her friend up. “Wow,” chimed in Cardi B. Hundreds of fans also sang Chloe’s praises when they laid eyes on her creative costume.

Speaking of Coach Meg, her Halloween costume has been making waves across the internet this week as well. The 26-year-old showed off a provocative Pinhead look earlier this week, and last night, she donned a black and white ensemble to portray Cruella DeVille.
