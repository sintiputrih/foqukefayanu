Early life

Omar Miles Gooding aka Omar Gooding was born on 19th October 1976, in Los Angeles, California, the United States of America. As of now, Omar is 47 years old and is a citizen of America. He has the zodiac sign of Libra. Talking about his parents, he is the son of  Cuba Gooding Sr, and his mother’s name is Shirley Gooding.
