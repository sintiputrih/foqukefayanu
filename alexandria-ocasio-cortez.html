Who is American Politician Alexandria Ocasio-Cortez?

Alexandria Ocasio-Cortez is an American activist, politician, and educator who has served as the U.S. Representative for the 14th congressional district since 2019 for New York as a member of the Democratic Party. Ocasio-Cortez became the youngest woman ever to serve in the United States Congress after taking office at 29.

Before becoming the youngest person elected to Congress, Ocasio-Cortez made headlines in 2018 by beating a 10-the-term New York Democrat incumbent in a congressional primary. On June 26, 2018, she drew national recognition when she won the Democratic Party’s primary election for New York’s 14th congressional district. On November 6, she emerged victorious in the election to become the youngest woman ever elected to Congress. She has always been very vocal on significant issues like health, education, inequality, environmental immigration, and tax policies.
