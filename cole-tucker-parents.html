Publish date: 2024-07-04

Who Are Cole Tucker Parents?: Cole Tucker, also known as Cole Bryson Tucker is an American professional baseball shortstop and outfielder.

He developed a passion for playing baseball at a younger age, has been consistent throughout his career, and has become one of the most sought-after baseball players

As of this writing (Tuesday, December 5, 2023), Tucker is a free agent. He previously played Major League Baseball for the Pittsburgh Pirates and Colorado Rockies.

Who Are Cole Tucker Parents?

Cole Tucker is the son of Erin and Jackie Tucker. Although he’s famous, we do not have any information (date of birth, age, education, and occupation) regarding his parents as of this writing. However, Both his parents have athletic backgrounds and are very supportive of their son’s career.

Cole Tucker was selected by the Pittsburgh Pirates in the first round, with the 24th overall selection, of the 2014 Major League Baseball draft.

He signed on June 12, 2014, for a signing bonus worth $1,800,000, and spent 2014 with the Gulf Coast League Pirates, where he batted .267 with a .724 OPS.

Tucker spent the 2015 season with the West Virginia Power where he posted a .293 batting average along with 25 stolen bases and 13 doubles

In 2017, he began the season with Bradenton Marauders and was promoted to the Altoona Curve in July of the same year (2017)

In a total of 110 games, Tucker posted a .275 batting average, six home runs, 50 RBIs, and 47 stolen bases in the 2017 campaign

After Tucker’s 2018 season, the Pittsburgh Pirates added him to their 40-man roster. He opened the 2019 season with the Indianapolis Indians.

In April 2019, he was called up to the major league roster, making his major league debut in a match against the San Francisco Giants.

In 2020, the Pirates played Tucker as an outfielder, slashing .211/.266/.361. He was later optioned Tucker to Indianapolis to start the 2021 season.

In May and July 2020, he played in 13 games for the Pirates and another 30 games after he was recalled in August. In 2021, he batted .222/.298/.342 in 43 games

In 2022, Tucker appeared in 18 games for the Pirates, playing second base, shortstop, and right field. He batted .175 and was optioned to Indianapolis on May 12.

On December 7, 2022, Tucker signed a minor league deal with the Colorado Rockies. He began the 2023 season with the Triple-A–Albuquerque Isotopes.

With the Triple–A Albuquerque Isotopes, Tucker played in 58 games, hitting .311/.420/.460 with 5 home runs and 34 RBI.

On August 7, 2023, the Rockies selected Tucker’s contract, adding him to the major league roster. In 3 games for Colorado, he went 3–for–7 with 2 RBI and a walk.

On October 4, 2023, Tucker became a free agent and in December 2023, he hit the headlines following his union with his partner, Vanessa Hudgens.
