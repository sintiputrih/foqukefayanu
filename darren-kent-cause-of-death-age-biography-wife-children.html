Darren Kent Cause of Death, Age, Biography, Wife , Children

Publish date: 2024-05-24

Darren Kent, known for his roles in EastEnders and Game Of Thrones, has died aged 36.

The actor battled and overcame osteoporosis and arthritis, reports the Mirror.

Kent was a patron of Equal People Performing Arts since 2018 with the founder of Mushroom Theatre Company, Penni Bubb, assigning him to the role.

Darren had also battled and overcome “an extremely rare skin disorder”, the newspaper reports.
